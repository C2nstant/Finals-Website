About

My World

Welcome to my Hardcore Minecraft Journal !

 

This world is played in Hardcore, which means that I only have one life and there are no second chances. Every day survived becomes a part of the story, and every build is a step toward leaving behind a lasting world.

Playstyle

I play with a medieval aesthetic in mind, so that means I try to avoid anything that breaks immersion. My goal is not just to survive, but to also create a world that feels like it could exist in a medieval fantasy. To achieve this, I have put restrictions on myself in this world.

 

Restrictions:

  • One Block At A Time: To keep the world authentic, I avoid automatic farms and large redstone machines. If I want food, I tend the fields myself. If I need resources, I mine or gather them directly. This makes progression slower, but also makes every crop harvested and every chest of resources feel earned.

  • Medieval Theme: All of my builds are inspired by medieval fantasy. I lean toward castles, villages, rustic farms, and fortresses rather than futuristic redstone contraptions.

  • Glitchless: I play this world glitchless, which means no exploits, bugs, or unintended mechanics are used.

Goals

The end goal I set for myself in this world is to complete every single advancement. For me, finishing all of them feels like the ultimate way to say I've done everything this world has to offer. But at the same time, I don't want this to just be about ticking boxes. The most important thing for me is to have fun along the way. Whether I make it all the way to the last advancement or not, I want to look back on this world and feel like the time I spent here was worth it.
